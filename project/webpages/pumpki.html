Pumpkin Detail

Pumpkin is a very nutritious vegetable with many health benefits. It is rich in vitamins A, C, and E, which help improve eyesight, boost immunity, and protect the body from infections. Pumpkin contains antioxidants that support heart health and reduce inflammation. It is high in fiber, which improves digestion and helps prevent constipation. Pumpkin is low in calories, so it is good for weight management. It also contains important minerals like potassium and magnesium that help control blood pressure and keep bones strong. Overall, pumpkin is a healthy food that supports overall body health.
