Milk Detail

Milk is a highly nutritious food that provides many health benefits. It is rich in calcium, which helps build strong bones and teeth and prevents osteoporosis. Milk contains high-quality protein that supports muscle growth and repair. It is a good source of vitamins such as vitamin D and B12, which help strengthen immunity and support brain and nerve function. Milk also provides energy and helps keep the body hydrated. Drinking milk regularly supports healthy growth in children and helps maintain overall body health in adults.
